# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Primary users are parking administrators and operators managing cameras, access rules, logs, and users in day-to-day operations.
They work in a live operational context where decisions must be quick and traceable.

## Product Purpose
Nomera provides an operational control panel for parking access workflows driven by plate recognition.
It helps teams manage parking lots and cameras, approve or reject access requests, monitor events, and keep visibility over system activity.
Success means reliable operator control with fewer manual bottlenecks and clear operational state at any moment.

## Brand Personality
The interface voice is calm, clear, and friendly.
It should reduce stress during routine operations, remain unambiguous under pressure, and feel approachable for frequent daily use.

## Anti-references
This should not look like a noisy dark-neon monitoring dashboard.
It should avoid over-styled marketing visuals and avoid dense, legacy enterprise clutter.

## Design Principles
- Operational clarity first: critical actions and system state stay immediately understandable.
- Calm control surface: visual hierarchy should lower cognitive load, not amplify urgency.
- Friendly precision: copy and interactions stay approachable while remaining exact.
- Traceable decisions: actions and outcomes are observable through logs and status context.
- Role-aware simplicity: show the right controls for each role with minimal friction.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Accessibility is not fully specified yet, so use sensible defaults:
- Target WCAG 2.1 AA contrast and readable type sizes for primary workflows.
- Ensure full keyboard navigation and visible focus for all interactive controls.
- Keep motion subtle and avoid motion-only meaning.
- Use clear labels and error messages for fast comprehension in operational settings.
